KARDS

Welcom to the paradise of card games

Go wild with card games!!

Strong competitor to an entertainment field with no market learder...

They say that it is important to tap into untapped scpaces. But our team believes that it is equally important to tap into unsuccessfully tapped spaces as well. Which is why we are introducing KARDS.

Unlike the other card game apps in the market, KARDS does not restrict itself to one kind of card games. Instead KARDS will be a super app in the card game industry. We will be launching the app with the most locally famous game known as OMI and with the time we plan to expand this globallyby integrating universal card gamesand presenting more of our local ones.

One of our aims being to create a virtual platform with new features for all to connect with each other, while playing your favourite card game.

What differentiates us from others?

Online Audio Call Feature

With KARDS, user can connect with friends and family at one's convenience for relaxation. Our audio call feature will undoubtedly make them feel closer and engaged with each other.

Connecting users without external apps

Until now, users needed another platform which facilitates voice/video call or chat facility that is capable of connecting two or more users to gether. You need not to worry anymore as KARDS enebles you to connect seamlessly without using any external apps.

No excess usage of RAM

There's no need of handling two apps at the same moment for just one purpose, which is really burdensome for both you and your device, since you can while gaming using only KARDS. (2 in 1)
