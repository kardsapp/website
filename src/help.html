How to play | OMI

When the game starts , players will be automatically assigned to teams.

Start round button will be displayed just to the round leader and touching cards will be disabled for the other players. Once the round leader starts the round by calling trumps, 4 cards will be given to each player . Then he has to press the Trump called button (He/She will be able to announce the trump to the other players) the other 4 cards will be given to each player .

When one plays the card (double tap to select a card) ,only his touching will be enabled.After each player plays their turn, touching cards will be disabled.

After the trick is over, the call trick button would appear on player screens . The winner of the trick should make sure to touch this button (the other members should refrain from touching this button) and he/she will be the one to call trumps in the next round and the cards will go to the player's side. At that moment touching screen will be enabled.

At the end of every round, a screen with three buttons GIVE, RETURN and CONTINUE will appear to two players(one from each team). This is to exchange keta or the score cards amongst the teams and the functions of the buttons are as follows: Give- Give one Keta to the opposing team Return- Take one Keta given by accident, once Continue- To proceed to the next round without any more exchanges

Note: For multiple exchanges click on the respective button multiple times.

Please note that KARDS is an interactive card game where you and your friends get the chance to play your favorite card games with a virtual card deck. Make sure to be thorough with the rules and play fairly so that all four will be able to enjoy the game equally!

Happy Playing! :)
